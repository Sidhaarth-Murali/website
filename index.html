Sidhaarth Sredharan Murali

Hi! I'm a final year B.Tech student at National Institute of Technology Surathkal, Karnataka, working on my major project under Dr. Anand Kumar M.

My research interests encompass computational linguistics with a focus on Q&A systems, multilingual processing, and multimodal AI applications, including text-to-image models and visual Q&A. I am particularly passionate about multimodal Q&A systems and optimizing learning from multimodal sources.

Research Experience: I have gained valuable research experience as an undergraduate researcher, working under Dr. Bhawana Rudra on machine learning and quantum machine learning system analyses, and under Dr.Anand Kumar M on AI text detection and Trojan detection in large language models (LLMs). Additionally, I interned in HALE Lab under Dr. Sowmya Kamath focusing on biomedical retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)-based question and answer systems.

During my internship at Exela Technologies in the Summer of 2023, I developed a PDF chatbot utilizing OpenAI APIs and OCR models for medical bill document summarization. Presently, I am a research intern at IBM IRL, working under the mentorship of Vishwajeet Kumar, Rudra Murthy V, and Jaydeen Sen. Here, I am focusing on the development of a retrieval-cum-generation model designed to excel in both representational and generative tasks.

Currently, I have published papers in Semeval 2024, ACL SRW 2024, ICCCNT 2024, and have papers under review in SecureComm 2024 and CoNLL 2024.
